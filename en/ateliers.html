The clay, the gesture, the table

At Franquette, ceramics are a natural extension of the plate: pieces shaped at the workshop end up on the restaurant’s tables and in the shop.

In small groups, come and learn hand-building and wheel throwing, or refine your technique over a coffee. No experience needed — just the desire to leave with a piece of your own.

Formats: beginner workshops, regular courses, private sessions (birthdays, team building).

How to book?
