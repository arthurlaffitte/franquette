The Franquette spirit

At Franquette, we choose products that are good, clean and fair, sourced from producer friends. A Slow Food philosophy, a festive atmosphere, and an address just 100 metres from Place de Clichy.

In the morning, the smell of freshly ground coffee. At noon, a sweet-and-savoury break in the sun. In the afternoon, clay spinning at the workshop. In the evening, chef Yass’s cosmopolitan tapas and natural wines. Always “à la bonne franquette”.
