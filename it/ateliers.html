L’argilla, il gesto, la tavola

At Franquette, ceramics are a natural extension of the plate: pieces shaped at the workshop end up on the restaurant’s tables and in the shop.

In small groups, come and learn hand-building and wheel throwing with Marie, our ceramicist, over a coffee. No experience needed — just the desire to leave with a piece of your own.

In practice: every other Sunday, 2-hour sessions led by Marie, in small groups. First workshops in October 2026 — places will go fast.
