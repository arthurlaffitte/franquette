Caffetteria mediterranea · martedì–sabato 8:30 – 17:30

Il sole al bancone

By day, Franquette becomes a coffee shop with Mediterranean accents. Quality coffees, beans selected from renowned roasters, brewed with care: espresso, latte, cappuccino, flat white.

For green and sweet tooths: matcha, Sode artigianali UMA, and a sweet-and-savoury snacking menu to enjoy at any hour — focaccia, treats and sun-drenched fruit.
